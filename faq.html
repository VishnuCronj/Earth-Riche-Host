FAQs of Earth Riche Exports

What products does Earth Riche Exports offer?

Earth Riche Exports specializes in the export of high-quality dry fruits sourced from trusted suppliers around the world. Our product range includes almonds, cashews, pistachios, walnuts, dried figs, raisins, and more.

How can I place an order?

Ordering from Earth Riche Exports is easy! Simply browse our website, select the desired products, and add them to your cart. Proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and choose your preferred payment method to complete the order.

Do you ship internationally?

What are your shipping options and delivery times?

We offer various shipping options to accommodate different delivery preferences and timelines. Shipping times may vary depending on your location & the chosen shipping method. Typically, orders are processed & dispatched within 7 business days, and delivery times range from 5 to 7 days.

Are your products organic and sustainably sourced?

Yes, at Earth Riche Exports, we are committed to offering organic and sustainably sourced dry fruits. We work closely with our suppliers to ensure that our products meet the highest quality standards while adhering to ethical and environmentally friendly practices.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods to provide convenience and flexibility to our customers. These include credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and other secure online payment platforms.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is dispatched, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing tracking information. You can use this information to track the status and whereabouts of your package until it reaches your doorstep.

What if I receive damaged or defective products?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. In the unlikely event that you receive damaged or defective products, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will gladly assist you and arrange for a replacement or refund as per our returns and exchange policy.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?

Yes, we cater to bulk and wholesale orders for businesses, retailers, and distributors looking to purchase our dry fruits in larger quantities. For inquiries regarding bulk pricing, product customization, or partnership opportunities, please contact our sales team.

How can I contact Earth Riche Exports for further assistance?

If you have any other questions, feedback, or concerns not addressed in this FAQ, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. You can contact our customer support team via email at earthricheexports@gmail.com or through our website's contact form. We're here to help!
